Services

Adrion Diving Centre was established in 2007 in Vlore, 🇦🇱. Since 2007 the center has been operating from Paradise Beach. Our diving center continues to offer high quality services to thousands of divers throughout the world. We offer diving excursions to certified divers, and training for all levels from beginners to experienced divers. People can get to know the enchantments of the sea safely and easily by doing their first dives with us. We are proud of our dedication to high safety standards, clean and comfortable facilities, and friendly, experienced staff. These factors, in combination with the cosmopolitan atmosphere and --> beauty of Vlora, have established us as one of the top diving centers in ALbania. For us, diving is a serious occupation, but also a way of life. We consider each diver's individual needs and desires to make everyone's dive experience special.

Our centre "Adrion"

has explored the locations in and around Vlora and we can assure you there is a lot more hidden beauty underneath the waters of Ionian and Adriatic sea that you will be stunned to explore once you allow us to guide and make you explore. We are the first Registered Dive Center in Vlora affiliated with Scuba School International, adhering the international standards of expertise, equipments and believe in "Safe diving Practices"..

Why Dive in Vlore

We in Vlore offer the easiest access to diving in Albania. The visibility is usually averages 2 to 30 meters, The dive site offers a wide variety of coral reefs, rock boulders , a rich variety of marine life. Our trainers are very friendly, caring and patient like most of the people of Gujarat who will help you provide the of the experience. With the help of our trainers one will be able to scuba dive with proper training prior to open sea dive.

Guidelines

We require that all divers plan their dives comply with the following guidelines. For any dive deeper than 40 metres, or any dive where decompression gases are used, a dive plan must be completed and approved by relevant Trawangan Tech staff. All gas mixes MUST be analysed, correctly labeled for MOD and signed and dated by the user. Fill station log must be signed. Maximum bottom PO2 will be 1.4 ATA or lower. Maximum decompression PO2 will be 1.6 ATA or less. All divers must carry sufficient gas to complete their dives within rule of thirds, or as appropriate for the dive plan.

Discover the underwater world!

The Open Water Diver (OWD) is the entry level diving certificate of the CMAS Association and your first step into the international diving world. With this certificate (credit card like plastic card with your information and your photo) you can dive all over the world. For teenagers of 10 to 14 years the "Junior Open Water Diver" is offered. CMAS 1* Your first step into weightlessness! The training for CMAS 1* is the first step into the diving world from 14 years. Junior 1*, 2*, 3* and basic certification can be credited.
